How to reproduce
----------------
Run "Supplementary R Codes/19_GBIF_download_submit.R" to submit a fresh
download (requires GBIF account credentials), then
"Supplementary R Codes/20_GBIF_download_fetch.R" once GBIF flags the
download as SUCCEEDED to fetch the .zip and produce the cleaned RData.

The cleaned dataset used in the manuscript is provided as
"SDM data/Psammodromus_GBIF_cleaned.RData" (post quality control:
geographic clipping to the Iberian Peninsula, deduplication, removal of
fossil and unverified records, year >= 1980).
